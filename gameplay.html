How to Play

A fast tactical TCG where four attributes shape every decision — and timing decides the cosmos.

Attributes Turn Structure Card Types Energy Zones & Traps Win Conditions

Core Attributes

Every hero has four attributes that scale effects and unlock timings.

  • Tactics (T)

    Sets trap limit (T), card draw bonus (T-2), and counter roll (d6 ≤ T).

  • Skill (S)

    +S×10% to Action effects and enables combo bonuses.

  • Resilience (R)

    Shield = R×10; Trap resist = R×5%.

  • Mysticism (M)

    Magic scaling = M×5%; if M ≥ 4: 1/turn Mystic Cancel (2 energy).

Why it matters

Builds are defined by attribute lines. High-T heroes dominate trap tempo; high-S spike damage windows; high-R grind with shields; high-M bend the stack with cancels.

Energy & Scaling

Base energy each turn: 3 + ceil(M / 2)

Attribute scaling applies to many effects (e.g., action damage, shields, cancels). Plan turns around when to open the stack, when to hold counters, and when to push for lethal.

  • S multiplies action potency (+S×10%).
  • R sets your survivability (Shield = R×10).
  • M fuels mystic effects (+M×5% and cancels if M≥4).
Example

If M = 4, base energy = 3 + ceil(4/2) = 5. You may also perform one Mystic Cancel this turn (cost 2 energy).

Zones & Trap Timing

  • Deck — draw from here.
  • Hand — play Actions/Allies; set Traps face-down.
  • Field — Allies & flipped Traps.
  • Discard — resolved/defeated cards.
  • Banish — removed from the game.

Trap timing windows:

  • Instant — open anytime (priority permitting).
  • Reaction — in response to specific triggers.
  • Persistent — remains active after flip.

Counter rolls use T (d6 ≤ T succeeds).

How to Win

  • Deplete the opponent’s Shield (R×10, plus effects), or
  • Mill their deck (they cannot draw).

Tactical timing, stack priority, and attribute scaling decide the endgame.
